Divine Mercy Project

Divine Mercy in the Whole World

The project "Divine Mercy in the Whole World" was born out of a desire to spread the messages of hope and love that Jesus gave to humanity through His revelations to Saint Faustina Kowalska, with the fundamental support and spiritual guidance of Blessed Michał Sopoćko. In an era marked by profound uncertainties, this initiative aims to make the inexhaustible source of Divine Mercy accessible to anyone, in every corner of the planet, breaking down both linguistic and geographical barriers.

Description of the Project

The ecosystem of this initiative is structured around the development of digital platforms, mobile applications, and a strong presence on social media. The goal is to create an interconnected network that allows the faithful to engage in daily prayer, deepen their understanding of spiritual writings, and stay updated on global initiatives. The project features:

Divine Mercy Android App

A fully native application designed to accompany the faithful in daily prayer. Developed with the latest smartphone technologies, the app includes a guided recitation of the Chaplet of the Divine Mercy with a professional audio engine that works seamlessly in the background (even when the screen is turned off). It also features automatic progress saving for the 9-day Novena and a local notification system for the solemn reminder at 3:00 PM (the Hour of Mercy). The application is designed to function entirely offline, ensuring access to sacred texts and meditations at any time without consuming data.

Merciful Jesus Website

Development and management of the multilingual web portal, conceived as a permanent spiritual and historical archive. In addition to gathering the life and writings of Saint Faustina, the site offers sections dedicated to the latest news and detailed historical insights into the sacred places of Lithuania (particularly Vilnius), where the most significant events of Mercy took place and where the original First Image was painted. The content is optimized for fluid and accessible navigation from any device.

Visit Website: www.gesumisericordioso.org

Social Media

The creation, curation, and constant updating of dedicated channels—currently active via our Facebook Page, Facebook Group, and Instagram profile. This social media presence keeps the community vibrant day after day, offering daily spiritual insights, dedicated graphics, and inspiring quotes extracted directly from the Diary of Saint Faustina Kowalska, fostering sharing and mutual support in prayer among members.

Future Developments

The ultimate goal of this project is to constantly expand and reach every culture. In our future plans, we intend to integrate new languages into both the website and the application, expanding access to an ever-increasing number of nations.

A fundamental step will be the systematic inclusion of the writings of Blessed Michał Sopoćko within our digital tools and social platforms, which are currently almost entirely unpublished or available exclusively in Polish. Furthermore, we are working to implement a live streaming system from Vilnius, offering the faithful worldwide the opportunity to pray via a direct video connection in front of the historical places of Mercy, thus creating a truly vibrant and active international community.
